LED Sign Boards

This board has LED display consists of many closely spaced light emitting diodes. This board will promote your business and give you a competitive advantage over your competitors. Your sales and revenue as well as the growth rate of your business will increase if you use these signs. Apart from this, LED Sign Boards have a plethora of other benefits. We offer custom LED Sign Boards with custom graphic designs.

Acrylic LED Sign Board

Waterproof and weather resistant, made of high quality cast acrylic, with noble finish. Acrylic signs manufactured in transparent, opaque and milled finish. Acrylic is translucent, so it is innovatively used for lighting effects such as wiping, smoothing and clarity. Check out one of our acrylic works created with LED for the USPA KIDS shop.

Digital Signage

This signage is a segment of electronic signage. Digital screens use technologies such as LCD, LED and e-paper to display advertisements and billboards. They can be found in public spaces, transportation systems (KSTRC, Indian Railways, etc.), museums, stadiums, stores, retail stores, hotels and resorts, restaurants, cafes, and corporate buildings, etc., and used for wayfinding, exhibitions, marketing, and outdoor advertising.
