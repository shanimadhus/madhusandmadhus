About us

Founded in 1999, MADHUS ADVERTISING COMPANY, set out itself to be a leading Advertising Company in Kollam as well as in Kerala. We are dealing with Corporate, Industrial, Commercial and Construction Industries for the last 23 years. We are doing all kinds of Advertisements like Newspaper, Hoardings, Unipole boards, Digital Video hoarding in various centers, Advertisements in KSRTC and Railway, etc. Our objective is to deliver advertisement in to the public which ensures good exposure.

  • Respond immediately to the changing needs of our clients
  • Achieve complete customer satisfaction

  • Maintain professional and ongoing communication to achieve the highest standard of performance
